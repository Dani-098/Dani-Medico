Why Dani Medico?

Founded with the spirit of helping the student community, Dani Medico understands the pressure of medical entrance exams. We noticed that many brilliant students struggle due to the high cost of private academies. We decided to change that.


Our platform brings together the best notes, province-specific content (Sindh, Punjab, KPK, Balochistan), and past papers in one organized dashboard. Whether you are preparing for AFNS, NUMS, or the national MDCAT, we are here to support you every step of the way.
